JDM

JDM typically stands for "Japanese Domestic Market." It refers to vehicles, parts, and accessories that are designed and produced specifically for the Japanese market. JDM products are often known for their high quality, performance, and unique features. Enthusiasts around the world often seek out JDM cars and parts for their distinctive design and superior engineering.
